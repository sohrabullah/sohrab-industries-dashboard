Complete Investor Guide

Making an Investment

📌 Example 1: Invest Rs. 50,000 in Summer Batch → Batch sales Rs. 80,000, expenses Rs. 20,000 → Net profit Rs. 60,000 → You get Rs. 30,000 (50%) → ROI = 60%
📌 Example 2: Invest Rs. 100,000 in Winter Batch → Batch sales Rs. 150,000, expenses Rs. 40,000 → Net profit Rs. 110,000 → You get Rs. 55,000 → ROI = 55%
🎯 Best Strategy: Invest in multiple batches to diversify risk. Start with smaller amounts, track performance, then increase investment in high-performing batches.

Referral System

📌 Example 1: Refer 5 friends who each invest Rs. 10,000 → You earn Rs. 5,000 (10% commission)
📌 Example 2: Refer a large investor who puts Rs. 500,000 → Your commission = Rs. 50,000
🎯 Best Strategy: Share your referral link on social media, WhatsApp groups, and with family. Use the share buttons above to post directly!

Withdrawing Profits

📌 Example 1: Available balance Rs. 25,000 → Request withdrawal via EasyPaisa → Approved within 3-5 business days
📌 Example 2: Monthly profit Rs. 15,000 credited → Withdraw Rs. 10,000 and reinvest Rs. 5,000
🎯 Best Strategy: Reinvest 50% of profits to compound growth. Keep a portion for regular withdrawals to maintain cash flow.

Live Chat Support

📌 Example 1: Question about batch status → Send message → Get real-time response from support
📌 Example 2: Need help with withdrawal → Chat with support to resolve issues quickly
🎯 Best Strategy: Use chat for urgent matters. For complex issues, provide screenshots and transaction IDs for faster resolution.

Analytics & Ledger

📌 Example 1: View investment growth chart → See which batches performed best
📌 Example 2: Check ledger statement → View all credits (investments, profits) and debits (withdrawals)
🎯 Best Strategy: Review analytics monthly. Identify top-performing batches and increase investment there. Track ROI to optimize portfolio.

Batch Progress Tracking

📌 Example 1: Batch shows "In Production" → Bulbs are being manufactured
📌 Example 2: Batch status "Sold" → All products sold, profit calculation in progress
🎯 Best Strategy: Monitor batch progress regularly. When batch reaches "Sold", expect profit distribution within 7-10 days.
