How to Use - Complete Investor Guide

Making an Investment

What it does: Invest money in our LED bulb production batches and earn 50% profit share.

📌 Practical Example 1:

You invest Rs. 50,000 in "Summer Batch 2025".

Batch Performance: Sales = Rs. 80,000 | Expenses = Rs. 20,000

Net Profit: Rs. 60,000

Your 50% Share: Rs. 30,000

ROI: 60% (Rs. 30,000 profit on Rs. 50,000 investment)

📌 Practical Example 2:

You invest Rs. 100,000 in "Winter Batch 2025".

Batch Performance: Sales = Rs. 150,000 | Expenses = Rs. 40,000

Net Profit: Rs. 110,000

Your 50% Share: Rs. 55,000

ROI: 55%

💰 Profit Calculation Formula:
Your Profit = (Batch Sales - Batch Expenses) × 50%
⚠️ Important: If batch makes a LOSS, you also bear 50% of that loss. Always review batch details before investing.
🎯 Best Strategy:
  • Start with smaller amounts (Rs. 10,000 - 25,000) to test performance
  • Track batch progress regularly through Analytics tab
  • Reinvest 50% of profits to compound growth
  • Diversify across multiple batches to spread risk
Tip: Higher investment amounts in a single batch = Higher absolute returns!

What it does: Track your investment batches through complete lifecycle stages.

1. In Production → 2. Completed → 3. Sold → 4. Profit Distributed
📌 Stage Details:

  • Completed: Production finished - Ready for sale
  • Sold: All products sold - Profit calculation in progress
  • Profit Distributed: Your share credited to balance!

Understanding Profit/Loss

📌 Profit Scenario:

Batch Sales: Rs. 100,000 | Expenses: Rs. 30,000

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does it take to get profit after batch is sold?

Profit is typically distributed within 7-10 business days after the batch status changes to "Sold". You'll receive a notification when profit is credited to your balance.

❓ What happens if a batch makes a loss?

Losses are shared equally (50%) between investors and Sohrab Industries. If a batch makes a loss, your share of the loss will be deducted from your available balance. This is why diversification across multiple batches is recommended.

❓ Can I withdraw my original investment amount anytime?

Original investments are locked until the batch completes its full cycle. Once the batch reaches "Profit Distributed" status, your original investment becomes part of your available balance and can be withdrawn.

❓ How do I become a Gold or VIP tier investor?

Tiers are automatically assigned based on your total investment:
- Bronze: 0+ (Default)
- Silver: Rs. 50,000+
- Gold: Rs. 200,000+
- VIP: Rs. 500,000+
Higher tiers unlock better benefits and exclusive batches!

❓ Is my investment safe?

Sohrab Industries is a registered business with transparent operations. All investments are backed by real LED bulb production and sales. However, like any business, profits depend on actual sales performance. We recommend diversifying investments and reviewing batch details before investing.
