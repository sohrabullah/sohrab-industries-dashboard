How to Use - Complete Investor Guide

Making an Investment

What it does: Invest money in our LED bulb production batches and earn 50% profit share.

📌 Example 1: You invest Rs. 50,000 in Summer Batch. Batch generates Rs. 80,000 sales with Rs. 20,000 expenses. Net profit = Rs. 60,000. You get Rs. 30,000 (50%). ROI = 60%
📌 Example 2: You invest Rs. 100,000 in Winter Batch. Batch sales Rs. 150,000, expenses Rs. 40,000. Net profit = Rs. 110,000. You get Rs. 55,000. ROI = 55%
🎯 Best Strategy: Invest in multiple batches to diversify risk. Start with smaller amounts, track batch performance, then increase investment in high-performing batches.

Referral System

What it does: Earn 10% commission when someone joins using your referral link and invests.

📌 Example 1: You refer 5 friends. Each invests Rs. 10,000. You earn Rs. 5,000 (10% of 50,000).
📌 Example 2: You refer a large investor who puts Rs. 500,000. Your commission = Rs. 50,000.
🎯 Best Strategy: Share your referral link on social media, WhatsApp groups, and with family. Explain the profit sharing model to attract serious investors.

Withdrawing Profits

What it does: Withdraw your earned profits anytime via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or Bank Transfer.

📌 Example 1: You have Rs. 25,000 available balance. Request withdrawal via EasyPaisa. Approved within 3-5 business days.
📌 Example 2: Monthly profit of Rs. 15,000 credited. You withdraw Rs. 10,000 and reinvest Rs. 5,000.
🎯 Best Strategy: Reinvest 50% of profits to compound growth. Keep a portion for regular withdrawals to maintain cash flow.

Live Chat Support

What it does: Direct messaging with Sohrab Industries support team for any queries.

📌 Example 1: You have a question about batch status. Send a message and get real-time response.
📌 Example 2: Need help with withdrawal? Chat with support to resolve issues quickly.
🎯 Best Strategy: Use chat for urgent matters. For complex issues, provide screenshots and transaction IDs for faster resolution.

Analytics & Ledger

What it does: Track your investment growth, profit trends, and complete financial statement.

📌 Example 1: View chart showing your investment growth over time. See which batches performed best.
📌 Example 2: Check ledger statement showing all credits (investments, profits) and debits (withdrawals).
🎯 Best Strategy: Review analytics monthly. Identify top-performing batches and increase investment there. Track ROI to optimize portfolio.

Batch Progress Tracking

What it does: Track your investment batches through Production → Completed → Sold → Profit Distributed stages.

📌 Example 1: Your batch shows "In Production" - bulbs are being manufactured.
📌 Example 2: Batch status "Sold" - all products sold, profit calculation in progress.
🎯 Best Strategy: Monitor batch progress regularly. When batch reaches "Sold", expect profit distribution within 7-10 days.

Trust & Legal Section

Investments are subject to business performance and market conditions. Minimum lock-in period: 6 months.

💰 Profit Sharing Rules (50%)

Net profit/loss is calculated as Total Sales - Total Expenses. Investor receives exactly 50% of net profit OR bears 50% of net loss.

⚠️ Risk Disclosure

Profit and loss depend on real business performance. Past performance does not guarantee future returns.

Withdrawal requests processed within 3-5 business days after approval. Minimum withdrawal: Rs. 1,000.
