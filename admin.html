How to Use - Complete Guide

Investments

What it does: Investors can invest money in our LED bulb production batches and earn 50% profit share.

📌 Example 1: Investor A invests Rs. 50,000 in Summer Batch. Batch generates Rs. 80,000 sales with Rs. 20,000 expenses. Net profit = Rs. 60,000. Investor gets Rs. 30,000 (50%). ROI = 60%
📌 Example 2: Investor B invests Rs. 100,000 in Winter Batch. Batch sales Rs. 150,000, expenses Rs. 40,000. Net profit = Rs. 110,000. Investor gets Rs. 55,000. ROI = 55%
🎯 Best Strategy: Invest in multiple batches to diversify risk. Start with smaller amounts, track batch performance, then increase investment in high-performing batches.

Referral System

What it does: Earn commission when someone joins using your referral link and invests.

📌 Example 1: You refer 5 friends. Each invests Rs. 10,000. With 10% commission, you earn Rs. 5,000 (10% of 50,000).
📌 Example 2: You refer a large investor who puts Rs. 500,000. Your commission = Rs. 50,000 (10%).
🎯 Best Strategy: Share your referral link on social media, WhatsApp groups, and with family. Explain the profit sharing model to attract serious investors.

Orders & Products

What it does: Buyers can purchase LED bulbs directly from our store. Orders get professional IDs like SI0001PK.

📌 Example 1: Customer places order for 10 LED bulbs. Order ID: SI0042PK. Status tracks from pending → processing → shipped → delivered.
📌 Example 2: Bulk order for 500 bulbs by a business. Special pricing applied. Track order progress in real-time.
🎯 Best Strategy: Offer bundle deals (buy 5 get 1 free). Use the Marketing Hub to share promotional images and campaigns during festive seasons.

Profit Withdrawals

What it does: Withdraw your earned profits anytime via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or Bank Transfer.

📌 Example 1: You have Rs. 25,000 available balance. Request withdrawal via EasyPaisa. Approved within 3-5 business days.
📌 Example 2: Monthly profit of Rs. 15,000 credited. You withdraw Rs. 10,000 and reinvest Rs. 5,000.
🎯 Best Strategy: Reinvest 50% of profits to compound growth. Keep a portion for regular withdrawals to maintain cash flow.

Live Chat Support

What it does: Direct messaging with Sohrab Industries support team for any queries.

📌 Example 1: You have a question about batch status. Send a message and get real-time response from admin.
📌 Example 2: Need help with withdrawal? Chat with support to resolve issues quickly.
🎯 Best Strategy: Use chat for urgent matters. For complex issues, provide screenshots and transaction IDs for faster resolution.

Analytics & Ledger

What it does: Track your investment growth, profit trends, and complete financial statement.

📌 Example 1: View chart showing your investment growth over time. See which batches performed best.
📌 Example 2: Check ledger statement showing all credits (investments, profits) and debits (withdrawals).
🎯 Best Strategy: Review analytics monthly. Identify top-performing batches and increase investment there. Track ROI to optimize portfolio.
